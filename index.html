about cardshirt

The CardShirt Company is a not-for-profit, student run business based out of the University of Louisville College of Business. Here at CardShirt, we are dedicated to increasing interest in U of L athletics and academics through the creation and sale of an annually themed t-shirt all while boosting student support and involvement. The company provides invaluable experience to students at the University of Louisville by providing the unique opportunity to run a real business while generating profits for student scholarships.
